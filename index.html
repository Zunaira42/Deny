The 2022 DeFi crash exposed a fundamental flaw.
  • The ecosystem suffered from a lack of institutional-grade risk management and an inability to assess borrower creditworthiness. Simple, poorly executed overcollateralization left and continues to leave significant returns on the table.
  • A group of private equity and tech professionals came together to solve this issue. Having worked in private debt they saw the potential of on-chain infrastructure for improving yields, but also the opportunity in integrating TradFi finance concepts into on-chain processes to improve risk management.
